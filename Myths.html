World Tree

The realms of this universe are held together by what is known as the World tree, its branches or roots reach out to each realm. Even if it is called the world tree the actual form or shape of the tree is not certain as it is usually seen in glimpses through dreams and the only actual physical evidence of it exists in the form of the resemblance of roots in some parts of Devorna. Even if the form or shape of the tree is unknown the branches can be mapped with what is called the Arcane veins as they are very similar to blood veins stretching out over the realms. The world consists of six realms each governed by its own rules and has its own people and countries.

Spark

When you die your soul will travel on the branches of the world tree trying to reach the top and while doing that the soul will slowly be consumed by the world tree until nothing more than the tiniest spark of life is left. At the top the spark is sent back to the world to ignite a new life.

Eternal Sparks

When you die your soul will travel on the branches of the world tree trying to reach the top and while doing that the soul will slowly be consumed by the world tree until nothing more than the tiniest spark of life is left. At the top the spark is sent back to the world to ignite a new life.

Returners

Returners are different, they are strong souls that don't become completely consumed while walking the branches and keep their memories even after reaching the top. When they return back to the living they can maintain some of those memories making the new life able to remember a past life.
