World Lore

The realms of this universe are held together by what is known as the World tree, its branches reach out to each realm apart from Devorna. Even if it is called the world tree the actual form or shape of the tree is not certain as it is usually seen in glimpses through dreams and the only actual physical evidence of it exists in the form of the resemblance of roots in some parts of Devorna. Even if the form or shape of the tree is unknown the branches can be mapped out and are called the Arcane veins as they are very similar to blood veins stretching out over the realms. The world consists of six realms each resting on the branches of the world tree Enara, each realm is governed by its own rules and has its own people and countries.
