Races of Athernis

Light Elestine

Background:

The Elestine are a race that are what people call angels, they are a bewinged race living among the gods but not necessarily as their subjects. They have a strong mind and are in full control of their own thoughts. Among the Elestine there are two types, the Light Elestine who live in the bright parts of Athernis and the Dark Elestine who live in the dark parts. While they are the same their differences make them seem like two different races.

Appearence:

If the Elestine is a light Elestine their skin are usually very pale and feels almost powdery like, their eyes have almost nonexistent pupils while still having an iris. The iris can be different colors from almost all spectrums but more commonly they are silver or blue. They often have long sharp nails and large wings that enable them to fly.

Behaviour:

The Elestine have sensitive skin and they normally do not like to be touched by others. The feathers in their wings are filled with Arcane energies which allows them to fly. While they do maneuver using their wings they can normally be seen hovering only by stretching them out and do not like landing as their bare feet would then touch the hard surface of the ground.

Average Age: 300 years

High Elves

Background:

High Elves are a branch from the Fey Elves that built settlements near humans and learned to coexist with them. Building cities they started to adapt to their surroundings and became what they are today.

Appearence:

High Elves are known for their sharp ears, much shorter than the others of their kin but still long compared to humans. They often have golden blonde or silver white hair, there are cases where a high elf has other colors but the most known high elf would have blonde hair and long hair that they are very proud of. The skin colors are often pale, and since they always take care of their beauty they often have long nails and a face untouched by battle. High elf dress often in light clothes such as silk in bright colors, they love anything that glimmers so crystals, gold and silver are often worn as jewelry. Heavy metals on the other hand, such as iron gives the high elves a chilling sensation that makes them feel uneasy and weak.

Behaviour:

Not at all as secluded as their relatives, high elves cherish their beauty and are not shy to show it for anyone who wishes to adore it. The high elves, unlike the others of their kin, does not shy away from the other races; instead you can often find them mingling with other races even though they often consider them a race of lesser status and beauty. They have a strong affection to beauty and light, often working in high positions and/or crafting of gold, jewelry and silk. Because of their long life span they are known for being expert craftsmen.

A typical house of a High elf is that of a bright wooden house with plenty of carved or painted decorations, the house would have many large windows to let as much sunlight in as possible with silken drapes to cover them if needed. A typical high elf does not enjoy living in cold environments and in harsh sleeping conditions.

Elves perceive time differently than other races, this is mainly due to their long life span. Similar to how some people won’t notice or rather forget when they do some everyday chores an elf will feel the same about the years that pass. This can result in elves being very selective about what they remember and it is not uncommon for them to not remember names of people they don’t find worthy of remembering. Through their meditation they can handle their memories and can remember things they have forgotten with this method.
